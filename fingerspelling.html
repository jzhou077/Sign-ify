Getting Started

Fingerspelling is an essential part of American Sign Language (ASL) that involves spelling out words using the ASL alphabet. On this webpage, you can practice and improve your fingerspelling skills with the help of machine learning technology. To use this feature, simply allow camera permissions on your device. Once loaded in, enter a word you'd like to fingerspell (up to 10 characters) in the text section and click the "spell" button to begin practicing! The word will appear above your camera, and the letters will light up when you correctly sign them. Note that motion letters (i.e. "j" and "z") are not supported.
